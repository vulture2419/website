About



I am a college student currently pursuing computer science as a favourite course. I love to grow and develop into a better version of myself everyday. In the year 2018, I started the journey of learning to code and took the thought of “developing” seriously.

It was not until 2018 that I took things more seriously. I realised that I could do more. Ever since then I really adopted the growth mindset. Studying and reading became my second nature and I started to organize my life to take more responsibilities of my own happiness.

One day I hope to be a part of a team that helps people in need. But for now, I’ll be doing my part of helping my audience with my post, sharing my journey and little knowledge I have, hoping it helps someone in need.
